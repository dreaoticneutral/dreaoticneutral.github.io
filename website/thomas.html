Thomas Lucas Flyer

Released 2025 / Poster Design

Description

During the 2024-2025 academic year, I worked with professor Israel Davis to bring a Chicago artist to do a few demos and give a lecture to students. We explored a few options and settled on Thomas Lucas, who is a friend of Davis'. We decided he would speak about cultural competence and professional practice and that he would also have short meetings where he would talk to them about what they've been doing and where they can go. I also designed two flyers to advertise his visit. The second poster was designed to be more basic and readable, while the first one sacrifices a bit of readability for aesthetic.
