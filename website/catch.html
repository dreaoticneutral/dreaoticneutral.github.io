Catch 22 Book Redesign

Released 2021 / Book Cover Design

Description

“That's some catch, that catch-22”

This is a redesign of the cover for Joseph Heller's Catch-22. This is actually one of my favorite books, which says a lot about me, but interestingly enough nothing about my taste in literature. I don't really have too much else to say about it, the main characters are a bomber unit which is where most of the visuals come from, though I used the color palette of the Hulu series.

The idea of a catch-22 is much more amusing than encountering one in the world. Speaking from experience A no-win dilemma whose solution is hard to achieve because of contradictory rules is actually quite frustrating to deal with.
