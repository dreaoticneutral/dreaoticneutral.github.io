F U S I O N

Released 2024 / Digital Art & Poster Design

Description

"Get open, get honest, invent yourselves together. That's F U S I O N" -Garnet. The most prominent fusions in the show all showcase this idea, to varying degrees.

This is a series of minimalist posters featuring some of the most prominent fusions on the show Steven Universe, embellished by a (relatively) iconic quote that I feel encapsulates their entire existence. It was inspired by a similar design I saw for Sugilite on redbubble, as well as a Youtube video I found when I was younger that I still watch every now and then. I decided to do ones in this style of the rest of the more prominent fusions in the show. It was originally gonna be the Crystal Gems but I scrapped it cos I couldn't choose a single, encapsulating, iconic quote for Amethyst. I'll do something else for them in the future.

Steven Universe had a lot of good ideas (and just as many bad ones but I digress), but the one that was the most captivating for me was the whole fusion thing. The idea of a fusion being a physical embodiment of the relationship of the components is, I don't wanna say genius, but it's something. A few steps short of profound maybe. Plus it's just cool.
