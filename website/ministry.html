Ministry in Motion

Released 2020 / Logo & Branding

Description

Creativity, poise, dignity, expression, and style. Those are the values we wish to instill in today's youth, as we create well rounded individuals, nurture their love for the arts, and help them develop into the leaders of tomorrow.

This is a logo and brand specification sheet for a made up not for profit business. I chose to create a performing arts academy because I have experience in the environment.

My mom danced with Endure Productions (now Endure Charities) in Chicago and instructed at Umbiance Academy in Virginia, among other things, and is close friends with the directors of both, and she would constantly drag me along with her so it's something I'm quite familiar with despite not really being a part of it myself. Also included are mockups of stationary and other merchandise that would be available to purchase at the school. Or maybe they'd be given away with admission. I dunno.
