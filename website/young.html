Young Life

Released 2020 / Logo & Branding

Description

This is the logo I designed for our youth ministry at Life Church International. Ministry being a bit of a loose term as there are only like, four of them. Honestly it's more of a youth group or maybe a squad or team, but I digress.

There are two main ideas I'm playing with here, both of them involving the Y. Firstly, the Y being a person symbolizing that the people, however few of them there may be, are what makes the ministry. Secondly, the Earth being in the... Is that a hand or is the whole thing an arm? It doesn't matter, either one works I guess. Anyway, it symbolizes that the future of the world is in the hands of the youth. For better or worse, hopefully for better.

The logo was printed on shirts for what I presume was Youth Sunday. But since I wasn't actually there, I don't really know
